BlogNest Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Sunday, Feb 4, 2024

Welcome to BlogNest! At BlogNest, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our blogging app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your username, and any other information you choose to provide.

1.2 Usage Information: We collect information about how you use BlogNest, such as the posts you create, the posts you view, and the interactions with other users.

1.3 Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access BlogNest, including the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Provide and Improve BlogNest: We use your information to operate, maintain, and enhance the functionality of BlogNest.

2.2 Communicate with You: We may use your email address to send you important updates, notifications, and newsletters.

2.3 Personalization: We use your information to personalize your experience on BlogNest, such as recommending posts or users.

3. Information Sharing

3.1 Public Content: Your posts and certain profile information may be visible to other BlogNest users.

3.2 Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers to help us operate, maintain, and improve BlogNest.

3.3 Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.

4. Your Choices

4.1 Deleting Your Account: You can delete your BlogNest account by contacting us at blognest@proton.me.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Your continued use of BlogNest after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at blognest@proton.me.

Thank you for trusting BlogNest with your information!
